About Me

Step into the world of Prerak Siddhpura, where code meets creativity. As a full-stack web developer with 2 years of experience, I transform ideas into dynamic digital solutions through a blend of technical expertise and innovative thinking.

My journey in development spans from Python—where I first discovered my passion for coding by creating games and calculators—to mastering modern technologies like Next.js, TypeScript, React, and PostgreSQL. Currently pursuing BSc-IT at LS Raheja College of Arts and Commerce, I balance academic growth with hands-on experience, having completed the Udemy Full Stack Web Development course focusing on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript fundamentals.

Beyond personal projects, I've successfully delivered client solutions including Web3Events, and I'm currently engaged with Leonard Solutions' frontend development. My proficiency extends to essential developer tools like Git and GitHub, while my ongoing exploration of Web3 and Meta technologies demonstrates my commitment to staying at the forefront of digital innovation.

Join me on this progressive journey where every line of code builds toward something greater. Together, let's turn your digital aspirations into reality through thoughtful development and cutting-edge solutions.
